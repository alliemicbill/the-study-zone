What is The Study Zone?
Study Zone is a convenient tool that lets you make a simple to-do list while listening to the music of your choice! This app/site aims to help you focus on any tasks that you are working on, such as studying or working, allowing you to be in the "zone". Most importantly, this place is what YOU make of it! Happy Studying!
